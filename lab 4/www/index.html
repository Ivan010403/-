NEWS

Nature does not have bad weather, you just need to anticipate changes in time, dress appropriately, take an umbrella with you or leave it at home. But, judging by the numerous anecdotes about weather forecasters, it is not so easy to find out in advance about rain, wind, thunderstorm or snowfall. Meteorology has developed significantly over the past decades, largely due to the ability to use modern technologies and instantly transmit the information received.

The task of the site is to obtain and apply new knowledge in the study of weather processes, informing ordinary citizens, government agencies and economic enterprises about weather changes and possible dangers. Therefore, the site reflects the most accurate and complete information coming from the first hand. Users of our site are proud of the accuracy of forecasts, a clear and user-friendly interface, the availability of all necessary information, the ability to view the forecast worldwide, the availability of applications, which simplifies use.
